My bio

I'm a mild-mannered reporter working for the Daily Bugle. There is nothing interesting about me. Nope, nothing out of the ordinary. Just a regular, white collar type of guy. A bit on the scrawny side if I've being honest, but I've been working out lately to put on a few pounds.
